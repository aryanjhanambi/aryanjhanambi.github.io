About Canera

Canera is an educational consultancy committed to guiding Indian students on their journey to international universities. We combine global insights with local understanding, ensuring every student feels confident and supported — from choosing their stream in high school to stepping into their dream campus abroad.

Whether it's stream selection, application strategy, or visa readiness, our expert support and personalized plans make sure you don’t miss a step.
